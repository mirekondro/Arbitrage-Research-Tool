## No Guarantee of Profit

The term "arbitrage" and phrases such as "guaranteed profit" or "risk-free
profit" in this software's output refer to a theoretical mathematical
relationship between prices at a given instant. **They do not guarantee any
actual profit.** Factors that can eliminate or reverse an apparent arbitrage
opportunity include, but are not limited to:

- Price movement between detection and order execution (slippage)
- Order partially or fully unfilled at the quoted price
- Platform fees higher than estimated
- Market resolution disputes or cancellations
- Platform withdrawal limits or delays
- Counterparty risk (platform insolvency, regulatory action)
- Internet latency or application errors
- Play-money platforms (Manifold, Metaculus) that cannot be converted to cash

**All use of this tool is at the user's sole risk.**
